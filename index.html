Internal Automation Tool

This application is an internal automation tool used to upload draft photo posts to TikTok for artist social media campaigns.

The application only accesses data required for TikTok authentication and content publishing. No personal data is sold or shared.

Content is uploaded as a draft and finalized manually by the creator in the TikTok app, including music selection.

This is not a consumer-facing application.
